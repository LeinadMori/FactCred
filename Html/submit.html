Submit A New Claim

  • What is a claim? → A statement that can be fact-checked.
  • Can I submit opinions? → No, stick to verifiable facts.
  • Do I need to add a source? → Not mandatory, but highly recommended.
  • What happens after submission? → Our team reviews and publishes with status.
  • Can I edit my claim after submission? → No, but you can submit a corrected version.
  • How long does review take? → Usually within 24–48 hours.
  • What kind of claims are not allowed? → Offensive, hateful, or non-verifiable statements.
  • Do I need to create an account? → Yes, for tracking your submissions.
  • Will my name appear publicly? → No, unless you choose to share it.
  • Can I submit claims in other languages? → Yes, we accept claims in multiple languages.

📌 Review Process

✅ Submit → 🕒 Review (24–48 hrs) → 📢 Published with status

🔍 FactCred values transparency: All claims are reviewed by independent fact-checkers to ensure accuracy.
